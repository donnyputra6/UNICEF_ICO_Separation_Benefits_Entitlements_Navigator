Your entitlements
Note on repatriation grant: Applicable only if your continuous active service is at least 5 years. Please check the Repatriation Grant Knowledge Article on the UNICEF intranet for the calculation method.
Note on termination indemnity: Applicable if continuous active service is at least 6 years and you sign the No Contest Agreement. Refer to the Separation Policy, Annex 1, page 22 for the calculation.
Important notes for all staff
  • Settle all debts before separation. Outstanding salary advance, rental advance, education grant advance, or other cash advances must be cleared with UNICEF Finance. UNJSPF will not process your pension benefits until all debts are settled.
  • Pension (UNJSPF). Obtain your UID by emailing requestuidonly@unjspf.org, then access your account at member.unjspf.org. Annual statements are available via the UNJSPF Member Self-Service portal.
  • Health insurance. Your CIGNA/Aetna coverage expires at the end of your last month of employment — not your last working day.
  • ASHI & ASLI. After-Service Health and Life Insurance eligibility depends on meeting retirement criteria and receiving a monthly pension benefit. Verify through the respective Knowledge Articles.
  • US citizens and residents. You are eligible for income tax reimbursement. Contact the UN Tax Unit at (212) 963-2949.
  • NYHQ staff retiring aged 55+. May be eligible to apply for a US Green Card. Contact visadesk@unicef.org and dosvc@un.org for eligibility and process.
